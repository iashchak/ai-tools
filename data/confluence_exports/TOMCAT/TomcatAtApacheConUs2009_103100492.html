Training & Session Schedule

The official schedule has been posted...

http://www.us.apachecon.com/c/acus2009/schedule/grid

There is a full day track of sessions on Wednesday, and two days of Tomcat training classes available on Monday & Tuesday

BOF / MeetUp - Monday, Nov 2rd @ 8PM

There will be a Tomcat MeetUp on Monday night at 8PM. This event is open to anyone who wants to come, even if you are not registered for the conference.

(Draft) Plan for the meetup:

  • Servlet 3.0
  • Servlet 3.0 and Tomcat 7.0
  • how to become a committer
  • security
  • memory leaks on reload
  • harnessing the power of CATALINA_BASE
  • Other?
